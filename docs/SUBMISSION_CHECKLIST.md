## 💡 PRO TIPS

### For Judges

1. **Lead with your strength**: 100% emergency accuracy is your killer metric
2. **Emphasize innovation**: True multi-agent architecture, not just prompt chaining
3. **Show production quality**: Complete testing, docs, and deployment strategy
4. **Highlight safety**: Clinical safety focus with red flag detection
5. **Demonstrate impact**: Clear path from prototype to real-world deployment

### Visual Presentation

1. **Use all 6 images**: Each tells a different part of your story
2. **Order matters**: Put architecture diagram early to explain your approach
3. **Consistent style**: All images use similar color schemes and fonts
4. **Professional quality**: High resolution, clean layouts, readable text

### Written Content

1. **Start strong**: Open with the problem and its importance
2. **Show results early**: Mention 100% emergency accuracy in first paragraph
3. **Be specific**: Use numbers (14 tests, 6 agents, 2.7M assessments)
4. **Balance technical and accessible**: Explain innovation clearly
5. **End with impact**: Remind judges of real-world potential
